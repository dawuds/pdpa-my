## 1. Notification Details
*   **Data Controller Name:** Retail-Co Malaysia Sdn Bhd
*   **Notification Date:** 2026-03-08 (within 72 hours of discovery)
*   **Discovery Date:** 2026-03-07 14:00 PM

## 2. Nature of the Breach
*   **Incident Type:** Unauthorized Access (API Vulnerability)
*   **Data Involved:** Name, Email, Mobile Number, Purchase History.
*   **Sensitive Data:** None (no credit card or password data compromised).
*   **Approx. Data Subjects:** 5,500

## 3. Likely Consequences
*   **Risk Level:** MEDIUM
*   **Potential Harm:** Increased risk of phishing and social engineering for affected customers.

## 4. Remedial Actions Taken
*   **Containment:** The vulnerable API endpoint was taken offline at 15:30 PM on 2026-03-07.
*   **Remediation:** Security patch deployed; all API keys rotated.
*   **Notification:** Direct email notification sent to all 5,500 data subjects on 2026-03-08.
