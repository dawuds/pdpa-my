## 1. Purpose and Scope

This document provides a suite of standardized consent form templates for use by [Organization Name] in the collection, processing, and sharing of personal data from customers, employees, research participants, and other data subjects. All templates are designed to satisfy the notice requirements under **Section 7** and consent validity standards under **Section 6** of the **Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)**.

These templates apply to all business units, channels, and data collection touchpoints operated by [Organization Name], including but not limited to digital platforms, branch operations, call centres, and third-party partnerships. The templates must be used in conjunction with the organization's Privacy Notice and Data Protection Policy.
