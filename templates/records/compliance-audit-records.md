## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document serves as the official record of data protection compliance audits conducted by or on behalf of **[Organization Name]** in accordance with the **Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)**, specifically Sections 5 (General Principle) and 9 (Security Principle). It provides a structured repository for audit programs, findings, risk ratings, management responses, remediation actions, and closure evidence to demonstrate ongoing regulatory compliance.

This record supports the organization's accountability obligations and enables the Data Protection Officer (DPO) and Board to assess the effectiveness of the personal data protection framework across all processing activities.

| Reference | Provision | Obligation Summary |
|---|---|---|
| PDPA 2010, s5 | General Principle | Personal data shall not be processed unless data subject has given consent and processing meets one or more prescribed conditions |

### Appendix A — Severity Rating Definitions

*The following severity ratings are applied to all audit findings. Ratings are determined based on the potential impact on data subjects, regulatory exposure, and likelihood of occurrence.*

| Rating | Definition | Required Response Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | Finding represents an active or imminent breach of PDPA obligations with significant potential for harm to data subjects or material regulatory penalty. Immediate management attention required. | Immediate action; remediation plan within **5 business days** |
| **High** | Finding represents a material gap in PDPA compliance with significant risk exposure. No current breach identified, but risk of one is elevated without prompt remediation. | Remediation plan within **15 business days**; completion within **60 days** |
| **Medium** | Finding represents a moderate compliance gap. Current controls partially effective but require improvement to fully meet PDPA requirements. | Remediation plan within **30 business days**; completion within **90 days** |
| **Low** | Finding represents a minor control deficiency or a best-practice recommendation where the risk of non-compliance is low. | Remediation at next scheduled review or within **180 days** |
| **Observation / Advisory** | No compliance gap identified; however, an opportunity for enhancement has been noted. No formal response required. | At management's discretion |

### Appendix E — Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Data Subject** | An individual who is the subject of personal data processed by the data controller |
| **Data Controller** | A person who either alone or jointly or in common with other persons processes any personal data, or has control over or authorizes the processing of any personal data |
