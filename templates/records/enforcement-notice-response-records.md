## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This document serves as the official record file for enforcement notices received by **[Organization Name]** from the Personal Data Protection Commissioner ("the Commissioner") pursuant to the **Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)**. It consolidates all materials related to the notice, the organization's legal assessment, the compliance roadmap, evidence of remedial actions, and all correspondence exchanged with the Commissioner throughout the compliance period.

This record is maintained to demonstrate that [Organization Name] has responded to enforcement action in a timely, transparent, and legally compliant manner, as required under PDPA **Sections 110, 111, and 133**.

### 1.2 Scope

This record applies to:

- All enforcement notices issued by the Commissioner to [Organization Name] under the PDPA 2010.
- All business units, functions, and third-party processors whose activities are the subject of the enforcement notice.
- All personnel involved in the response, remediation, and correspondence activities arising from the notice.

### 1.3 Regulatory Basis

| Legislation | Section | Provision |
|---|---|---|
| Personal Data Protection Act 2010 | s110 | Power of Commissioner to issue enforcement notice |
| Personal Data Protection Act 2010 | s111 | Requirements and effect of enforcement notice |
| Personal Data Protection Act 2010 | s133 | Offences and penalties for non-compliance with enforcement notice |
