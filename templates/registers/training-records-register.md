## 1. Purpose and Scope

### 1.1 Purpose

This Training Records Register documents and maintains evidence of data protection training completions for all personnel within **[Organization Name]**. It supports the organization's obligation to demonstrate staff awareness and competency in personal data handling, as required under the **Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)**, specifically Section 9 (Security Principle).

This register serves as the authoritative record for audit, regulatory inspection, and internal governance purposes, evidencing that [Organization Name] has taken reasonable steps to ensure that persons processing personal data are aware of their obligations and have received adequate training.

### 1.2 Scope

This register applies to:

- **All permanent employees** of [Organization Name], regardless of role or seniority
- **Contract staff and temporary employees** engaged for periods exceeding [specify duration, e.g., 30 days]
- **Third-party processors and vendors** who handle personal data on behalf of [Organization Name] under a data processing agreement
- **New joiners** prior to or within [specify timeframe, e.g., 30 days] of commencing duties involving personal data
- **Board members and senior management** with oversight responsibilities for data governance

### 1.3 Out of Scope

*Identify any categories of personnel or training programmes excluded from this register.*

- [ ] [Any exclusions, e.g., contractors engaged solely for non-data-related physical services]
- [ ] Training records covered under separate frameworks (e.g., Anti-Money Laundering, information security certifications) unless those programmes include a PDPA/data protection component
