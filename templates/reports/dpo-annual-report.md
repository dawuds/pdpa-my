## 2. Purpose and Scope

### 2.1 Purpose

This DPO Annual Report is prepared pursuant to **Section 129B of the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA)** and fulfils the statutory obligation of the Data Protection Officer (DPO) to report to [Organisation Name]'s senior management and board on the performance of DPO functions and the organisation's personal data protection compliance standing.

The report provides the Board of Directors and Senior Management Committee with:

- A comprehensive account of all DPO activities undertaken during the reporting period;
- An objective assessment of [Organisation Name]'s compliance posture against applicable PDPA obligations;
- Documentation of significant events including data breaches, regulatory engagements, and enforcement actions;
- Evidence of the effectiveness of the personal data protection programme; and
- Recommendations for resource allocation, policy decisions, and strategic direction to strengthen the organisation's data protection framework.

This report also serves as a primary audit artefact demonstrating to the Personal Data Protection Commissioner (**PDPC**) that [Organisation Name] maintains a functioning, adequately resourced, and independent DPO function as required under the PDPA.

### Appendix J — Glossary of Terms

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|

| **PDPC** | Personal Data Protection Commissioner — the regulator established under the PDPA. |
