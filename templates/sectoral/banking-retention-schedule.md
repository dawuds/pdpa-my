## 1. Statutory Basis
This schedule aligns with the **Code of Practice for the Banking and Financial Institutions Sector** and statutory requirements under FSA/IFSA.

## 2. Retention Periods

| Category of Data | Retention Period | Justification |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Customer Identification (KYC)** | 7 Years post-account closure | AMLA 2001 & BNM Requirements |
| **Transaction Records** | 7 Years from date of transaction | FSA 2013 Section 145 |
| **Credit Assessment Records** | 7 Years from date of assessment | Limitation Act 1953 |
| **Direct Marketing Consent** | Until withdrawal + 2 years | PDPA Retention Principle |
| **CCTV Footage (Bank Premises)** | 30 - 90 Days | Physical Security Standards |

## 3. Disposal Method
*   **Physical:** Industrial shredding (cross-cut).
*   **Electronic:** Secure cryptographic erasure or physical destruction of media.
